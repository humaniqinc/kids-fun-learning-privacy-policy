        <li>Photos or videos</li>
        <li>Location data</li>

    </ul>

    <h2>Advertising</h2>
    <p>Our app contains advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads are configured to be age-appropriate and comply with Google’s policies for child-directed content.</p>
    
    <h3>Ad Settings:</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>We use AdMob’s child-directed treatment setting</li>
        <li>Ads are limited to age-appropriate content</li>

    </ul>
