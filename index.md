
# Privacy Policy for Kids Fun Learning

**Last Updated: December 9, 2025**

## Introduction

Kids Fun Learning ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information from users of our mobile application.

## COPPA Compliance

Our app is designed for children aged 1-8 years. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect:
- Personal identification information (name, address, email, phone number)
- Photos or videos
- Location data
- Contacts
- Any other personal data from children

### Information We May Collect:
- **Anonymous Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous, aggregated data about how the app is used (e.g., which activities are played) to improve the app experience.
- **Device Information:** Basic technical information such as device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes.

## Advertising

Our app contains advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads are configured to be age-appropriate and comply with Google's policies for child-directed content.

### Ad Settings:
- We use AdMob's child-directed treatment setting
- Ads are limited to age-appropriate content
- Personalized ads are disabled for all users
- No behavioral advertising or tracking

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob:** For displaying advertisements (configured for COPPA compliance)
- All third-party services are configured to comply with COPPA requirements

## Parental Controls

We implement a parental gate that requires adult verification before:
- Accessing settings
- Viewing ads for rewards
- Accessing external links
- Managing child profiles

## Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited data we do process.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
